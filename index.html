About Nutrition

Nutrition is important for everyone. When combined with being physically active and maintaining a healthy weight, eating well is an excellent way to help your body stay strong and healthy. If you have a history of breast cancer or are currently undergoing treatment, eating well is especially important for you. What you eat can affect your immune system.Good nutrition is much more than something to fill your stomach.The most important feature of a good diet is variety.We all know variety is the spice of life.Eating the right mix of vitamins and minerals will help you feel and look your best at any age.

The availability of healthy, affordable foods contributes to a person’s diet and risk of related chronic diseases. For more information on why good nutrition matters, what CDC is doing to make healthy eating easier for all people, and how we are making a difference, explore the options below.

Nutritionists use ideas from molecular biology, biochemistry, and genetics to understand how nutrients affect the human body. Nutrition also focuses on how people can use dietary choices to reduce the risk of disease, what happens if a person has too much or too little of a nutrient, and how allergies work.

This article will explain the different nutrients a person needs and why. It will also look at the role of the dietitian and the nutritionists.
